INDIAN GRANITE TILES

Granite is an igneous rock that can form in any array of colors depending on its mineralogy and quartz composition. Indian Granite has become a point of interest in real estate decoration for its use in kitchens and bathrooms as counter top surfaces especially due to is durability and appearance. Granite is a hard and very tough stone that has been used for centuries in construction and monuments.
