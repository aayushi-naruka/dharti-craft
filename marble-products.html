INDIAN MARBLE SLAB

Marble is a type of metamorphic rock. This ageless material is the basis of architectural wonders, artistic masterpieces and beautiful living spaces. Indian marble Slabs are variety of colors and processed with best state of art machines. Marble slab & Marble tile especially are one of the most versatile stone. Its elegance and durability make it a prime choice for many and varied uses. However, the main uses of marble are in construction, interior decoration and sculpture.

INDIAN GREEN MARBLE

is basically green with light or dark veins. Certainly the color and veining texture may change from quarry to quarry. Further the base color also changes from light to medium and dark green stone.

LADY ONYX MARBLE

Onyx is a type of marble where light can pass translucently. Onyx is available in Green tone and Pink tone. This is basically white onyx marble with ocean like waves of Green and pink tone. The back ground of onyx marble is white.

MERCURY BLACK MARBLE

is especially a wavy pattern marble. Similarly The waves are mix of black, white, light grey which enhances the beauty of the marble slab to the highest level.

POLAR WHITE MARBLE

is a white base marble with lite color veins, waves and accent of light grey. Moreover Marble takes good polish. However Polar White is known for exclusive appearance and great texture.

RAINFOREST BROWN MARBLE

has light to medium brown hues of color with tree branch like veins of dark brown and accents of white. Furthermore Characteristics in veining and movement vary between lots, depending on block to block.

RAINFOREST GREEN MARBLE

has light to medium brown hues with tree branch looking veins of brown and green color. Further accents of white adds to the beauty. Furthermore Characteristics in veining and movement vary between lots, depending on block to block.

FOREST YELLOW MARBLE

has light to medium brown hues with tree branch looking veins of dark brown and accents of white.Furthermore Characteristics in veining and movement vary between lots, depending on block to block.
