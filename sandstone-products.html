INDIAN SANDSTONE SLABS

The Indian Sandstone paving is a natural stone on earth crust found in India with different strength and colors. We supply various stone Paving slabs exterior, Garden, flooring tiles and wall cladding. Further Indian Sandstone paving Slabs is used in various applications like flooring, facade, wall cladding, paving slabs, interior exterior. In Addition stone fountain, statues, Gazebos, stone pebbles, Pebblestone, Cobble Stones and sandstone patio..

AGRA RED SANDSTONE

is a pure red color Agra stone and donates color of fire as per Science of Architecture. Therefore Agra Red Sandstone adds high energy level to Prestigious Building façade and paving. Especially Red India Stone is eye catching and attracts people.

BLACK SANDSTONE

is a perfect choice displaying a dark grey background that is beautifully enhanced with subtle white shading. Moreover 22mm Calibrated Sandstone paving and Black Indian Sandstone paving slabs are preferably used for all type of construction flooring.

AUTUMN BROWN SANDSTONE

is a light brown Sandstone. Certainly Autumn brown Sandstone is very strong and dense sandstone mainly used for paving. Further Brown sandstone is mainly processed in Natural cleft top finish, hand dressed edges and thickness (Back) is calibrated to 22 mm.

CAMEL DUST SANDSTONE

is a strong Indian Sandstone paving with pleasing tones. It is soft creamy light yellowish brown. In addition, also have gentle random hues of golden brown running through this natural paving slabs. Yellow Buff, camel dust are the relevant popular name of Buff Paving Slabs.

DHOLPUR BEIGE SANDSTONE

is processed in tiles and slabs of various finishes. For example Natural, Sawn, Honed, Shot-blasted and leather. Moreover The Sawn and Honed finish of beige stone are used for interior flooring and building façade.

KANDLA GREY SANDSTONE

is a Grey stone extremely strong Indian sandstone. Especially the Hardness is due to the quartz grains being cemented together with silica and calcite. Furthermore it shares the solid modern feel of blue stone and looks simply stunning, when laid around pool.

MINT SANDSTONE

is available in Natural split, Honed and Sandblasted finishes. Indian Paving slabs is also processed for pool coping and step tread formats. Furthermore, Mint white is a white color Indian Patio Slabs with little effect of yellow or mint color.

TEAKWOOD SANDSTONE

The grain of the wood for which it is named as Teakwood. It is a soft stone with little variation in color and texture from one quarry to another. Moreover some are very light yellow, some are little dark. The combination of dark brown, golden veins make it a perfect choice for the home owners.
