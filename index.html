ABOUT US

Nagendra Naruka founded Dharti Craft in 1996 and expanded the company in the Stone sector with strong dedicated work force. Dharti Craft has more than 24 years of experience in exports. We primarily exports MARBLE, GRANITE, SANDSTONE, SLATE, SEMI-PRECIOUS STONES, LANDSCAPING STONES, STONE VENEER SHEETS, PEBBLES, etc.

Our professionalism and the vast range of stones in a multitude of finishes that we export in our USP. The customer comes first for us. Our motto is to supply high-quality Indian Stone at affordable prices.

During the course of our journey of nearly 24 years, we have faced many ups and downs, but nothing diverted us from our path. Instead, we took every problem as a challenge and learned a new lesson while overcoming the same.
